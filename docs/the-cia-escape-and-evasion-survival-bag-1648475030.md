# 中央情报局逃脱和逃避生存袋

> 原文:[https://life hacker . com/the-CIA-escape-and-escape-survival-bag-1648475030](https://lifehacker.com/the-cia-escape-and-evasion-survival-bag-1648475030)

中情局特工要做好一切准备，有时这意味着依靠有限的资源生存。他们的生存工具包很小，但包括出城住一段时间所需的一切。

Watch

这个包看起来像一个 [Maxpedition M-2 腰包](http://www.maxpedition.com/store/pc/M-2-WAISTPACK-p152.htm) 。这是里面的内容:

*   [莱泽曼水枪 PS4](http://www.amazon.com/Leatherman-831195-Squirt-Black-Keychain/dp/B0032Y2OT6/ref=sr_1_1?asc_campaign=InlineText&asc_refurl=https://lifehacker.com/the-cia-escape-and-evasion-survival-bag-1648475030&asc_source=&ie=UTF8&keywords=leatherman squirt ps4&qid=1413826382&sr=8-1&tag=kinjalifehackerlink-20)T3】
*   [细线锁摘 13](http://www.lockpickshop.com/SLS-13.html)
*   [拉力扳手短扭伸缩](http://www.lockpickshop.com/TW-02.html)
*   [全向发光迷你 A 型发光棒](http://www.omniglow.com/direct/omniglow-1-1-2-inch-individually-foiled-glow-stick-red-50-per-bag.html)
*   [Suunto Clipper](http://www.amazon.com/Suunto-Clipper-NH-Compass-SS004102011/dp/B00BTO184I/ref=sr_1_1?asc_campaign=InlineText&asc_refurl=https://lifehacker.com/the-cia-escape-and-evasion-survival-bag-1648475030&asc_source=&ie=UTF8&keywords=suunto clipper&qid=1413826394&sr=8-1&tag=kinjalifehackerlink-20)T3】
*   [光子 x 光微光](http://www.amazon.com/Photon-Clear-X-Light-Micro-XMW/dp/B000LNGA2I/ref=sr_1_1?asc_campaign=InlineText&asc_refurl=https://lifehacker.com/the-cia-escape-and-evasion-survival-bag-1648475030&asc_source=&ie=UTF8&keywords=photon x-light micro light&qid=1413826450&sr=8-1&tag=kinjalifehackerlink-20)

还有一些他们通常不会包括在照片中的东西，包括切割金属的钻石线、轻便的捕鱼设备和陶瓷刀片。
